Reflections

In the section, we have learned how Information Security Management is essential for all businesses and organisations, and how it makes a big difference for any infrastructure architect and the organisation structure. Although each security role has the skills to focus on one side from the infrastructure side and depends on it, the cybersecurity team should have high-level skilled engineers who can put the security standards and understand the risks for all the company departments to protect the business from the business any attackers.

By using the four main principles of Information Security Management methodology(Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability, and Non-Repudiation), cybersecurity team can make sure they have a secure system from securing the data for public access and unauthorised users, to secure the business assets and avoid any change on it, then to check the availability of the information and deny any security attack attempt. Finally, follow the business standards to action any issue and fix it.

In addition to it, the Security team main work is to focus on leaking security points in the infrastructure and make sure all departments are working very closely with all vendors to validate and priories any vulnerabilities in the infrastructure. Furthermore, the security team should use risk assessment tools to help them in the remediation plan. Finally, if critical vulnerabilities are found, then the security team should make sure the stockholder and service owner are aware of it and work rapidly to mitigate the risks.
